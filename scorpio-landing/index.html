Welcome to Scorpio S11 Experience

Book, Explore, and Ride the powerful Scorpio S11.
The Mahindra Scorpio S11 is the top-end variant of the Scorpio,
featuring a 2.2L mHawk diesel engine and available in a 7-seater configuration.
It comes with a 6-speed manual transmission and offers a mileage of 15 kmpl.
The S11 boasts features like a touchscreen infotainment system, automatic climate control, and alloy wheels.

Top Features

    Key Specifications and Features:
  • Engine: 2.2L mHawk diesel engine.
  • Transmission: 6-speed manual.
  • Drivetrain: Rear-wheel drive (RWD).
  • Seating Capacity: 7-seater.
  • Mileage (ARAI): 15 kmpl.
  • Fuel Tank Capacity: 60 litres.
  • Wheels: Alloy wheels.

    • Exterior: Projector headlamps, new vertically stacked LED tail lights, dual-tone cladding, and integrated spoiler.

    Interior: Touchscreen infotainment system, automatic climate control, height-adjustable driver's seat, and front and rear armrests.

    Safety Features: Cruise control, gear shift indicator.

    Other notable features: Electrically adjustable ORVMs, rear wiper and washer, fog lights, and roof rails.
